This add on allows you to cycle through your news routine without having to keep changing the url.

The sites you like to visit should be listed above seperated each by a new line.

Press the 'Save' button once you have your list. Also be sure to include the whole link.

Use these navigation commands anytime to move the current tab on to the next page:

  • 'Alt+Shift+Right' - Next page
  • 'Alt+Shift+Left' - Previous page
  • 'Alt+Shift+Space' - Restart Routine to the first link

Enjoy your routine!
